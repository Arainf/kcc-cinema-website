About Us

Koronadal Commercial Corporation

ABOUT US KCC Malls, the leading shopping destinations in South Central Mindanao is strategically located in the heart of fast growing cities: General Santos, Koronadal, and Zamboanga City. Boasting of having more than three hundred different shops and establishments, we give you the utmost quality shopping experience from department stores, entertainment, to your dining escapades.

Accessibility going to the mall is not a concern since our malls are located just a few blocks from main highway of General Santos City and even fronting the national highway for Koronadal branch. KCC Mall de Zamboanga are just few steps away from Zamboanga City's airport. Reaching us can be so convenient around town by means of jeepneys, multi-cabs, buses, tricycles and taxis which are readily available.

Our mall houses different shops and establishments, ready to cater to the various needs of our dear customers from signature brands of apparels and clothing to the latest fashion trends , accessories, household needs, fixtures, appliances to music and gadgets, sports equipments and whole lot more. All at most competitive price to fit your daily budgets. Sale and Price Drop Off are just some of malls varied activities that most shoppers await because of a wide range selections of items and big big discounts for almost all items.

We boast of our word-class facilities giving our dear customers a world-class shopping experience, entertainment and convenience from escalators, to walkators and elevators which are available at the place where they needed them most. We assure our dear customers of the quality and high sanitation compliance of our malls by hiring top-caliber food personnel to prepare cuisines that are affordable for the market. Furthermore, clean and modern restrooms are also spread out within the mall for easy access and convenience.
