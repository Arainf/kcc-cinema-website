The Nun II

In 1956 France, Sister Irene is called upon by the Vatican to investigate the murder of a priest at a boarding school. She soon discovers that the school is haunted by a malevolent force in the form of a demonic nun, Valak.

Irene teams up with a young nun named Debra and a former farmer named Maurice, who is possessed by Valak, to confront the demon and save the school. Along the way, they uncover a dark secret about the boarding school and Valak's true motive.

In the end, Irene and Debra are able to defeat Valak and banish her back to hell. Maurice is also freed from her possession, and the school is saved.
